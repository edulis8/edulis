About the Owner

Eric was born and raised on the California coast, in Santa Barbara, and traced a slow line up it as he studied social change in Santa Cruz and worked farming and gardening in Pescadero and San Francisco. Wanderlust brought him to English teaching gigs in Chile, backpacking in China and Laos, and two years in the Peace Corps in the Dominican Republic. His heart never forgot that it belonged coastside, and in 2014 he settled down in Arroyo Grande with his wife Xanat and their chihuahua named Maximo, and devoted himself to working the sweet sixteen acres owned by his father.
